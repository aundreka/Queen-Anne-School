Scholarship Programs

Academic Excellence

For students with outstanding academic performance (90% and above average).

Up to 100% tuition discount

Requirements:

  • General average of 90% or higher
  • No grade below 85% in any subject
  • Good moral character certificate
  • Maintain performance throughout the year

Athletic Scholarship

For students excelling in sports like basketball, volleyball, badminton, and martial arts.

50-75% tuition discount

Requirements:

  • Proven athletic ability and achievements
  • Commitment to school teams
  • Maintain academic standing
  • Attend all training sessions

Loyalty Scholarship

For students who have been with Queen Anne School since Pre-School.

20% tuition discount

Requirements:

  • Enrolled since Pre-School/Kindergarten
  • Consistent enrollment record
  • Good academic and behavior record
  • No major disciplinary issues

City Scholar Program

Partnership with local government for deserving students.

Full tuition coverage

Requirements:

  • Resident of Sta. Rosa or Dasmariñas
  • Meet income requirements
  • Academic excellence demonstrated
  • Community service involvement

DepEd ESC

Education Service Contracting program for Junior High students.

₱9,000/year subsidy

Requirements:

  • Grade 7 students eligible
  • Meet DepEd ESC criteria
  • Complete application process
  • Annual renewal required

Sibling Discount

For families with multiple children enrolled at Queen Anne School.

10% discount per sibling

Requirements:

  • Two or more siblings enrolled
  • Applied to younger sibling's tuition
  • Both students in good standing
  • Automatic application

Required Documents

For New Students

  • Birth Certificate (NSO/PSA issued)
  • Report Card/Form 138 (Previous school)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Medical Certificate
  • 2x2 ID Pictures (4 pieces)
  • Recommendation Letter (if applicable)

For Transferees

  • Form 137 (Student's Permanent Record)
  • Certificate of Transfer Credential
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate (NSO/PSA issued)
  • Medical Certificate
  • 2x2 ID Pictures (4 pieces)

Important Note

All documents must be original copies or certified true copies. Photocopies will not be accepted unless properly authenticated.
