Directions by Car

From Manila: Take SLEX, exit at Santa Rosa. Follow signs to New Santa Rosa Homes subdivision.

From Laguna: Take the national highway toward Sta. Rosa, turn into New Santa Rosa Homes.

Landmarks: Near Enchanted Kingdom and Santa Rosa City Hall

Public Transportation

From Manila: Take bus to Sta. Rosa via EDSA or Buendia, then jeepney to New Santa Rosa Homes

From Laguna: Take jeepney or UV Express to Sta. Rosa proper, then tricycle to the school

Note: School service available for enrolled students

Parking Information

• Free parking available on campus

• Separate areas for visitors and staff

• Security personnel on duty during school hours

• Additional parking during special events

Frequently Asked Questions

Our admission requirements vary by grade level. Generally, we require birth certificates, previous report cards, medical certificates, and good moral character certificates. For transferees, we also need Form 137 and certificate of transfer credential. Please contact our admissions office for specific requirements for your child's grade level.

Our enrollment typically starts in February and continues through May for the following school year. However, we accept applications year-round, especially for Pre-School. Early enrollment is encouraged as slots are limited. Please check our website or call our admissions office for specific dates.

Yes! We offer various scholarship programs including Academic Excellence (up to 100% tuition discount), Athletic Scholarships (50-75% discount), Loyalty Scholarships (20% discount), City Scholar Program (full coverage), DepEd ESC subsidies, and Sibling Discounts. Each scholarship has specific requirements and application processes.

We offer over 25 clubs and organizations including sports teams (basketball, volleyball, badminton, karate, chess), performing arts (chorale, dance troupe, drama club), academic clubs (science, math olympiad, debate society), and community service groups. Our teams have won numerous championships at regional and national levels.

Absolutely! We encourage prospective families to visit our campus. You can schedule a tour by calling our admissions office at (049) 502-0370, emailing us, or using our contact form. Tours are available Monday through Saturday during school hours, and we can also arrange special appointments outside regular hours if needed.
