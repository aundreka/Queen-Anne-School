About Queen Anne School

Our Story

Founded in 1994 by Mrs. Aster B. Atutubo with just 48 Pre-School students at Mariquita Pueblo II, Queen Anne School has grown into a premier educational institution serving students from Pre-School to Senior High School.

Our commitment to holistic education has remained steadfast through the years, as we continue to shape compassionate, competent leaders rooted in faith and service.

Since 1994

Our Journey

1994

Founded by Mrs. Aster B. Atutubo at Mariquita Pueblo II with 48 Pre-School students

1995–96

Tripled enrollment; added Elementary (Grades 1–6)

1996–97

Relocated to 2,783 sqm lot at New Santa Rosa Homes

1997–98

Opened High School; received DepEd recognition

2025

Launching TESDA Training & Assessment Center
